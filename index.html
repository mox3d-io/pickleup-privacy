Privacy Policy for PickleUp: Pickleball Scorer

Effective Date: April 4, 2026 | Last Updated: April 4, 2026

Summary: PickleUp does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app operates entirely offline with all data stored locally on your device. We have no servers, no analytics, no ads, and no way to access your information. The app may optionally open external services (such as Google Maps or your device's share sheet) at your request, but no data is sent automatically.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how PickleUp: Pickleball Scorer ("PickleUp," "the App," "we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use our Android application. We are committed to protecting your privacy, and we have designed PickleUp to operate with zero data collection.

2. Information We Collect

We collect no information whatsoever.

PickleUp does not collect, request, or access any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or hardware information
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Cookies or tracking technologies
  • Photos, contacts, or other device data
  • Financial or payment information (in-app purchases are handled entirely by Google Play)

3. Data Storage

All data you create within PickleUp -- including match scores, player names, stats, and practice logs -- is stored exclusively in a local database on your device. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is never transmitted over the internet
  • Is never accessible to us or any third party
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app or clear the app's data through your device settings

4. Internet Access

PickleUp does not require an internet connection for its core functionality. All scoring, statistics, and match data are processed and stored locally on your device. However, certain optional features may open external applications or websites at your request, including:

  • Opening Google Maps to search for nearby pickleball courts
  • Sharing match results or tournament brackets via your device's share sheet
  • Future optional in-app purchases handled entirely by Google Play

These actions are always user-initiated. No data is sent to our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

PickleUp does not embed any third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, or tracking. The app may open external applications at your request, such as:

  • Google Maps (for court discovery)
  • Your device's default sharing apps (for sharing match results)

These interactions are handled by your device's operating system and are governed by those applications' own privacy policies. The only other third-party interaction is through the Google Play Store for app distribution and optional in-app purchases. Google Play's own privacy practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Advertising

PickleUp currently contains no advertisements. We do not use any advertising SDKs or ad networks. If advertising is introduced in a future version, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly and users will be notified.

7. User Accounts

PickleUp does not require or offer user accounts. There is no login, registration, or authentication of any kind.

8. Children's Privacy

Because PickleUp collects no personal data of any kind, the app is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction) -- indeed, we do not collect information from anyone.

PickleUp complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) provisions for children, and similar laws worldwide by virtue of collecting no data at all.

9. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not have access to any data. When you use the app's share feature to send match results, this action is initiated and controlled entirely by you through your device's standard sharing mechanism.

10. Data Security

Your locally stored data is protected by your device's own security measures (screen lock, encryption, etc.). Since no data is transmitted from your device, there is no risk of interception during transmission.

11. Your Rights

Since we collect no personal data, there is no data for us to provide, correct, or delete. You have full control over all data stored locally on your device and can delete it at any time by:

  • Clearing the app's data in your device settings
  • Uninstalling the app

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated "Last Updated" date. If we ever introduce features that involve data collection -- which we currently have no plans to do -- we will update this policy in advance and, if appropriate, request your consent.

13. Google Play Data Safety

In accordance with Google Play's Data Safety requirements, we declare that PickleUp:

  • Does not collect any user data
  • Does not share any user data with third parties
  • Does not use data for any purpose
  • Does not process data in any way

14. Applicable Law

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with applicable privacy laws and regulations worldwide, including but not limited to the GDPR (EU), CCPA/CPRA (California), LGPD (Brazil), and PIPEDA (Canada). Because PickleUp collects no data, the app inherently satisfies the requirements of these and other privacy frameworks.
